EXPERIMENT NO:1

REALIZATION OF FUNCTIONS USING BASIC AND UNIVERSAL GATES(SOP AND POS)

AIM:

To familiarize with logic gates IC packages and to notify the truth table of logic gates. Also to

familiarize with the digital IC trainer kit.

COMPONENTS AND EQUIPMENTS REQUIRED:

IC 7400,7402,7432,7486 and IC trainer kit

THEORY:

IC trainer kit: IC trainer kit is an equipment mainly used to test and set up digital circuit

while doing experiments. Integrated circuits can be fitted in sockets or bread provided in it. The associated circuits can be set up on the breadboard. There are built-in voltage sources and block signals

in it. The frequency of the clock can be selected by turning the knob into different positions. In order to

feed monopulses manually, a debouncer switch is also provided. A number of select switches are also provided

to obtain 0 or 1 state voltages for digital inputs. Green and red LEDs are provided to represent low and

high states respectively to visualize the digital inputs.

TROUBLESHOOTING WITH DIGITAL IC:

Experiments with digital ICs are rather easy when compared with analogue integrated circuits. Operators

must be careful about the connecting wires and pins of IC. In most logic gates, pin no.07 is ground (GND)

and pin no.14 is Vcc. But this is not the case with all the digital ICs. There is a chance that the

operator may commit a mistake by assuming that pin no.07 and pin no.14 are GND and Vcc

respectively for all 14 pin ICs.

LOGIC GATES

In digital electronics, a gate is a logic circuit with one output and one or more inputs. Logic gates

are available as ICs.

AND GATE: The AND gate performs logical multiplication, more commonly known as AND operation.

The AND gate output will be high only when all the inputs are in high states. 7408 is a digital IC

in the TTL family and contains four AND gates.

OR GATE: It performs logical addition; its output will become high if any of the inputs is

logically high. 7432 is quad two input OR gates.

NOT GATE: It performs a basic logic function called inversion or complement. The purpose

of the inverter is to change one logical level to the opposite level. IC 7404 is a hex inverter.

NAND GATE: A NOT gate following an AND is called NAND or NOT AND. Its output will be low if

all the inputs are in high states. 7400 IC is a quad two input NAND gate.

NOR GATE: A NOT gate following an OR gate is called NOR or NOT OR. Its output will be low if

any one of its inputs is in a high state. 7402 IC is a quad two input NOR gate.

XOR GATE: Its output will be high if and only if one input is in a high state. 7486 is a quad

two input XOR gate.

PROCEDURE:

1. Test all the components and IC packages using a logic digital IC tester. Also, ensure whether all

the connecting wires are in good condition. The continuity of wires can be tested using a trainer kit

by shorting a 5V supply in the trainer to an LED of the panel. If wires are in good condition, the LED will glow.

2. Verify the dual-in-line package (DIP) pinout of the IC before feeding the inputs.

3. Set up the circuits and observe the outputs. Enter the input and output states in truth tables

corresponding to the input combinations.

RESULT:

Familiarized with the logic gates and verified truth tables. Also familiarized with the IC trainer kit.
